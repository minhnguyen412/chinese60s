COLLOCATIONS

有（一）点儿 means "slightly" (mostly used to refer to something unpleasant.)

有（一）点儿 + ADJ

The meaning of the interjection “啊” ("ah") varies with the change of its tones. When pronounced in the 4th tone it means "I see" or "Now I understand". For example:

啊，知道了。(Ah, I see!)

The modal particle “呢” is used at the end of an indicative sentence to indicate confirmation.
