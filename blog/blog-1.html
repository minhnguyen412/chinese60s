How Chinese60s Helps You Learn Smarter

1. Watch Structured Dialogue Videos

Each lesson starts with a video based on a standard teaching curriculum. The conversations are crafted to match beginner to intermediate levels—so you’re not overwhelmed, but still learning naturally.

Why it works: You train your ears, recognize sentence patterns, and practice pronunciation in a controlled environment.

2. Study the Conversation Line by Line

Right below the video, the dialogue is broken down line by line for you.

  • Click the arrow icon to reveal meaning and pinyin
  • Click the headphone icon to hear the audio for each line

This makes it easy to listen, read, and speak—all in one step.

3. Learn Every Word and Character in Depth

Click on any Chinese character to unlock:

  • A clear explanation of the word
  • A related image to help you visualize and remember
  • The correct pronunciation

This builds a stronger vocabulary foundation that sticks.

4. Understand Grammar Instantly

Click the star icon next to any sentence to see a grammar breakdown tailored to the sentence.

No more forum searches or confusing Google results—everything you need is already there.

5. Practice Writing Chinese Characters

Chinese60s includes a built-in space where you can practice writing characters.

It helps you:

  • Learn correct stroke order
  • Improve your writing skills
  • Boost your memory through handwriting
