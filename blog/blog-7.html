[Real-Life Chinese] SPA 按摩 - SPA Massage - Douyin Script

If you’ve ever wondered what a SPA massage is, or want to learn how native Chinese speakers describe relaxation and wellness — this post is for you!

Let’s explore the video script together 👇

📽️ The Script: SPA 按摩 - SPA Massage

Original Chinese:

SPA按摩是啥意思，你知道吗？
许多人不明白SPA按摩是什么意思。
SPA按摩呢？
首先进入到有音乐伴奏、
充满香味灯光柔和的房间。
晚上养生师为您准备好的一次性衣服。
躺在专门的推拿SPA床上。
第二呢，
一位受过专业培训的养生师带着特别的精油来到房间。
及时的与您沟通后进行专业的精油按摩服务。
是以嗅觉、清洁和触觉为一体的按摩养生盛宴。
它能放松减压，让你在身心愉悦中解除疲乏。
你懂了吗？

Pinyin:

SPA ànmó shì shá yìsi, nǐ zhīdào ma?
Xǔduō rén bù míngbái SPA ànmó shì shénme yìsi.
SPA ànmó ne?
Shǒuxiān jìnrù dào yǒu yīnyuè bànzòu,
chōngmǎn xiāngwèi, dēngguāng róuhé de fángjiān.
Wǎnshàng yǎngshēng shī wèi nín zhǔnbèi hǎo de yīcìxìng yīfú.
Tǎng zài zhuānmén de tuīná SPA chuáng shàng.
Dì èr ne,
yī wèi shòuguò zhuānyè péixùn de yǎngshēng shī dàizhe tèbié de jīngyóu láidào fángjiān.
Jíshí de yǔ nín gōutōng hòu jìnxíng zhuānyè de jīngyóu ànmó fúwù.
Shì yǐ xiùjué, qīngjié hé chùjué wéi yītǐ de ànmó yǎngshēng shèngyàn.
Tā néng fàngsōng jiǎn yā, ràng nǐ zài shēnxīn yúyuè zhōng jiěchú pífá.
Nǐ dǒngle ma?

English Translation:

What does “SPA massage” mean? Do you know?
Many people don’t understand what SPA massage really means.
So, what is it exactly?
First, you enter a room with soft music,
filled with fragrance and gentle lighting.
In the evening, a wellness therapist prepares disposable clothes for you.
You lie down on a professional massage bed.
Next,
a professionally trained therapist comes in with special essential oils.
After communicating with you, they begin a personalized essential oil massage.
It’s a sensory feast that blends smell, cleanliness, and touch.
It helps you relax, relieve stress, and eliminate fatigue in both body and mind.
Got it now?

📚 Grammar Structures to Notice

Grammar Point Explanation Example
啥意思 (shá yìsi) Colloquial for “什么” 这个词是啥意思？
许多 (xǔduō) More formal than 很多 许多人不知道这个地方。
进入到 (jìnrù dào) Emphasizes atmosphere/space 进入到房间后，他开始打扫。
的 + Noun Modifier Forms adjective phrases 专业的按摩
为 + sb + Verb Indicates action for someone 他为我准备了早餐。
Measure Words (量词) 次, 位, 场 etc. 一位养生师, 一场盛宴
