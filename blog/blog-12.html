[Real-Life Chinese] 刚梦到真的一个让特别让人害怕的梦 - I just dreamed a really, really scary dream - Douyin Script

In this lesson, we will study a script where someone describes a dream that felt frightening and strange. The style is casual and conversational—like a friend telling you a story. This is a great way to practice listening comprehension and learn how native speakers naturally express themselves.

We will go through the script line by line, provide pinyin, English translation, explain grammar points, and highlight key vocabulary.

Let’s explore the video script together 👇

📽️ The Script: 她的厨艺真的是可以的 - Her cooking is really great

Script with Pinyin and Translation

刚刚梦到真的一个让特别让人害怕的梦。
Gānggāng mèngdào zhēnde yī gè ràng tèbié ràng rén hàipà de mèng.
I just dreamed a really scary dream.

梦到脸这里长了一个长长的蘑菇还是痘痘吧。
Mèngdào liǎn zhèlǐ zhǎng le yī gè chángcháng de mógu háishi dòudòu ba.
I dreamed that on my face here grew a long mushroom—or maybe a pimple.

像蘑菇但是又像痘痘。
Xiàng mógu dànshì yòu xiàng dòudòu.
It looked like a mushroom, but also like a pimple.

长得长长的这么长。
Zhǎng de chángcháng de zhème cháng.
It grew so long, really this long.

然后嗯我就擦药，擦到底部它的根部，这有擦到它的顶端。
Ránhòu, ēn, wǒ jiù cā yào, cā dào dǐbù tā de gēnbù, zhè yǒu cā dào tā de dǐngduān.
Then I applied some medicine, rubbing it from the bottom, its root, up to the very top.

我在想着我为什么擦药它还是越长越长呢。
Wǒ zài xiǎngzhe wǒ wèishénme cā yào tā háishi yuè zhǎng yuè zhǎng ne.
I was thinking, why does it keep growing longer even though I applied medicine?

我就特别害怕。
Wǒ jiù tèbié hàipà.
I got really scared.

然后脸上其他地方也开始长那种痘痘。
Ránhòu liǎn shàng qítā dìfāng yě kāishǐ zhǎng nà zhǒng dòudòu.
Then other places on my face also started growing those pimples.

我在想我是不是这个药有什么问题啊。
Wǒ zài xiǎng wǒ shì bú shì zhège yào yǒu shénme wèntí a.
I was wondering if there’s something wrong with the medicine.

我怎么越擦越多呀。越擦越严重。
Wǒ zěnme yuè cā yuè duō ya. Yuè cā yuè yánzhòng.
How come the more I apply it, the more pimples appear? The more I use it, the worse it gets.

我在想着拍照片给大家看一下。
Wǒ zài xiǎngzhe pāi zhàopiàn gěi dàjiā kàn yīxià.
I thought of taking a picture to show everyone.

发一个视频给大家看一下。
Fā yī gè shìpín gěi dàjiā kàn yīxià.
Send a video for everyone to see.

我录下来可是当时的灯光太昏暗我就想着算了。
Wǒ lù xiàlái kěshì dāngshí de dēngguāng tài hūn’àn, wǒ jiù xiǎngzhe suàn le.
I recorded it, but the lighting was too dim, so I thought, forget it.

然后当时我的舍友也回来了。
Ránhòu dāngshí wǒ de shèyǒu yě huílái le.
Then at that time my roommate also came back.

然后我还想着问他们怎么回事。
Ránhòu wǒ hái xiǎngzhe wèn tāmen zěnme huí shì.
Then I even thought of asking them what was going on.

但他们也好像是对着这个东西，感觉很正常的事情。
Dàn tāmen yě hǎoxiàng shì duìzhe zhège dōngxi, gǎnjué hěn zhèngcháng de shìqíng.
But they seemed to look at it as if it was totally normal.

哇当时我那个心里我感觉我完了。
Wa, dāngshí wǒ nà gè xīn lǐ, wǒ gǎnjué wǒ wán le.
Wow, at that moment in my heart I felt like I was done for.

真的还好醒来什么都没有。
Zhēnde hái hǎo xǐnglái shénme dōu méiyǒu.
Thankfully, when I woke up, nothing was there.

脸上很光滑。
Liǎn shàng hěn guānghuá.
My face was very smooth.

就是痘痘这里感觉这个脓可以挤了。
Jiùshì dòudòu zhèlǐ gǎnjué zhège nóng kěyǐ jǐ le.
It’s just that this pimple here felt like the pus could be squeezed out.

我今天就要把它挤掉可以吗？
Wǒ jīntiān jiù yào bǎ tā jǐ diào, kěyǐ ma?
Today I really want to squeeze it out, okay?

📚 Grammar Structures to Notice

  • 刚刚 (gānggāng) – means “just now.” Often used at the beginning of sentences.
    Example: 我刚刚醒来 (I just woke up)
  • V + 到 – shows result or completion of an action.
    Example: 梦到奇怪的事 (dream about strange things) / 擦到脸上 (apply onto face)
  • 让 + Subject + Adj/V – “make someone…”
    Example: 这张照片让人害怕 (this photo makes people scared)
  • 得 (de) – used after verbs to describe manner.
    Example: 他长得高高的 (he grows tall)
  • 越…越… – “the more…the more…”
    Example: 越擦越多 (the more I apply, the more appear)
  • 呢 (ne) – softens tone, often in questions.
    Example: 你在做什么呢？ (what are you doing?)
  • 拍 / 发 + 照片/视频 + 给大家看一下 – “take/send and show to everyone,” colloquial structure.
    Example: 拍照片给大家看一下 (take a photo and show everyone)
  • 录下来 (lù xiàlái) – to record and keep.
    Example: 把视频录下来 (record the video and keep it)
  • 把 + Object + Verb + Result – highlights how an object is changed/handled.
    Example: 把它挤掉 (squeeze it out)
