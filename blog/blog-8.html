[Real-Life Chinese] 治愈时 - Healing Time - Douyin Script

When learning Chinese, real-life scripts from native speakers are goldmines. Today, we’ll explore a Douyin video where a creator shares her body skincare routine, filled with vivid vocabulary, trendy expressions, and useful grammar patterns.

Let’s explore the video script together 👇

📽️ The Script: 护肤 - Skincare

Original Chinese:

Hello老婆们!
属于我们女生的治愈时光，打开魔法沉浸式护肤啦!
秋天是我最喜欢的季节，想把音乐节奏都放慢一些。
去澳门工作时买的漱口水，薄荷味迅速能唤醒起床气。
最近买的东西都是大地色的。
这木梳子造型感很强。
长梳齿按摩头皮更舒服。
自制的心形冰块真的太有爱。
仪式感从来都是可以自己给自己的。
果酱般的身体磨砂膏，滴点金胶精油混合滋润。
这种DIY护肤就很治愈。
太爱这琥珀色的沐浴油，每次戳泡泡都特别解压。
一般都是刷刷手肘膝盖关节位。
不同部位都得好好呵护。
维秘的香味真的很好闻。

Pinyin:

Hello lǎopómen!
Shǔyú wǒmen nǚshēng de zhìyù shíguāng, dǎkāi mófǎ chénjìn shì hùfū la!
Qiūtiān shì wǒ zuì xǐhuān de jìjié, xiǎng bǎ yīnyuè jiézòu dōu fàng màn yīxiē.
Qù àomén gōngzuò shí mǎi de shù kǒushuǐ, bòhé wèi xùnsù néng huànxǐng qǐchuáng qì.
Zuìjìn mǎi de dōngxī dū shì dàdì sè de.
Zhè mù shūzǐ zàoxíng gǎn hěn qiáng.
Zhǎng shū chǐ ànmó tóupí gèng shūfú.
Zìzhì de xīn xíng bīng kuài zhēn de tài yǒu ài.
Yíshì gǎn cónglái dōu shì kěyǐ zìjǐ jǐ zìjǐ de.
Guǒjiàng bān de shēntǐ móshā gāo, dī diǎn jīn jiāo jīngyóu hùnhé zīrùn.
Zhè zhǒng DIY hùfū jiù hěn zhìyù.
Tài ài zhè hǔpò sè de mùyù yóu, měi cì chuō pào pào dōu tèbié jiěyā.
Yībān dōu shì shuā shuā shǒu zhǒu xīgài guānjié wèi.
Bùtóng bùwèi dōu dé hǎohǎo hēhù.
Wéi mì de xiāngwèi zhēn de hěn hǎo wén.

English Translation:

Hello, ladies!
It’s time for our healing moments—let’s open the magic and dive into immersive skincare!
Autumn is my favorite season; I just want to slow down the rhythm of life a bit.
The mouthwash I bought when I was working in Macau—the mint flavor instantly wakes me up from morning grogginess.
Lately, everything I’ve been buying is in earthy tones.
This wooden comb has such a strong sense of design.
The long comb teeth give an even more comfortable scalp massage.
The homemade heart-shaped ice cubes are just so lovely.
A sense of ritual is something you can always give yourself.
Jam-like body scrub, with a drop of golden essential oil mixed in for nourishment.
This kind of DIY skincare is truly healing.
I absolutely adore this amber-colored shower oil—popping the bubbles is so stress-relieving every time.
Usually, I brush it over elbows, knees, and joints.
Every part of the body deserves good care.
The scent from Victoria’s Secret is just wonderful.

📚 Grammar Structures to Notice

  • 属于 – "to belong to" → 属于我们女生的治愈时光 (“healing time that belongs to us girls”)
  • 打开 – "to open" → 打开魔法沉浸式护肤 (“open the magical immersive skincare”)
  • 把 – structure for moving or affecting an object → 想把音乐节奏都放慢一些
  • 放慢一些 – “slow down a bit” — 一些 here softens the degree.
  • 真的 – intensifier (“really”) → 真的太有爱
  • 种 – classifier for type → 这种DIY护肤
  • 一般 – usually / normally → 一般都是刷刷手肘膝盖关节位
